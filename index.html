Microsoft

Microsoft Corporation is a multinational technology company based in Redmond, Washington, United States. It was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen and has since grown to become one of the world's largest software makers. Microsoft is known for its flagship products such as the Windows operating system, Microsoft Office suite, and the Xbox gaming console. It also provides cloud services through its Azure platform, and owns professional social networking site LinkedIn. Microsoft has a significant presence in the technology industry and is constantly innovating to create new products and services.
